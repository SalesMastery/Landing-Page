The Process

THE 3-PHASE BUILD

A structured rollout from diagnosis to a fully managed, scaling operation

01
Diagnose & Architect
Weeks 1–2
Full access audit: CRM, call recordings, KPIs, scripts, and market data
Mission, values & culture assessment call to identify where standards slipped
We build ICP profiles and offer-to-pain maps so reps sell from understanding
We reverse-engineer revenue targets into daily activity benchmarks per rep
Your dedicated sales manager audits rep skill levels, call quality, and identifies bottlenecks across the sales cycle
We build a custom 30-day ramp-up game plan and present it on a strategy call
02
Install the Operating System
Weeks 2–4
We define role-specific KPIs and roll out BOD/EOD reporting for all reps
We optimize CRM pipeline stages and enforce data hygiene rules
We build a live team leaderboard — performance and competition made visible
Your sales manager trains the team on ICP, offer breakdown, mission, values, and new standards
We lock in recurring call rhythms and set up backend automations
03
Manage & Scale
Week 4 Onward
Your dedicated sales manager runs the weekly rhythm: 1 team call, 1 closer call, 1 setter call + training-mode where needed
Data-driven problem solving — we fix process before touching scripts
We continuously refine objection handling and follow-up sequences from real call data
We monitor KPIs, reporting, and rep health scores — upgrade or replace seats when needed
Quarterly Scale Lab reviews: full QBR, re-reverse-engineer targets, and reset standards
